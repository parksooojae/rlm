--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B: [INDEPENDENT - Sequential Chain]

A farmer has rabbits. On day 1, there are 2 rabbits. 
Each day: every rabbit produces 1 new rabbit (population doubles), 
THEN 20% of the total population escapes (rounded down).

B = Population after day 8

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Find the sum of all prime numbers between 50 and 150 (inclusive) that,

C = Sum of qualifying primes

D = (A mod 100) × (B mod 10) + 17

- If the current number is even: divide by 2
- If the current number is odd: add the digit sum of the number

E = Result after 4 operations

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FINAL ANSWER: [DEPENDS ON D AND E]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FINAL = D + E + 42
